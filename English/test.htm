Find your product key

Your product key is a 25-character alphanumeric string, shown in five groups of five characters each, separated by hyphens. The location of the product key varies depending on how you acquired the product.

If you purchased a retail copy of the product on CD    The product key is either on a sticker on the CD sleeve or on an insert in the product package.
If you purchased the product on DVD    The product key is on the DVD package.
If you downloaded the product from the Symantec Store    The product key is stored on your computer as part of the download process and is included in the confirmation email from the Symantec Store.
If your computer came with the product already installed    You are provided with a product key as part of the activation process or you will find the key inside your packaging. Be sure to save your product key, as you will need it if you ever want to reinstall your product. You can save the key by printing it or, if your product supports Norton Account, by storing it in your Norton Account.
If you received a product key card    The product key is printed on the card along with instructions on how to use it. Be sure to save your product key by creating or signing in to your Norton Account. You will need it if you ever want to reinstall the product.
If you have stored your product key in your Norton Account    To recover your product key from Norton Account:
  1. Write down the following steps, and then click here
  2. In the following screen, sign in to your Norton Account by typing your email address and password.
  3. On the product keys screen, you will find your product key.
